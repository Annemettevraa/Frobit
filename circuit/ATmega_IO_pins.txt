Used IO pins

PB0		LED			(RoboCard LED)
PB1		Encoder left B		(OC1A) 			Purple
PB2		Motor right D 		(OC1B/SS) 
PB3		Motor right C		(OC2A/MOSI) 
PB4		Encoder left A		(MISO, INT4) 		Blue
PB5		Motor left D		(SCK)
PB6		OSC
PB7		OSC

PC0		Voltage measurement	(ADC0)
PC1		-			(ADC1)
PC2		-			(ADC2)
PC3		20ms toggle		(ADC3)
PC4		Encoder right A		(ADC4, INT12)		Blue
PC5		Encoder right B		(ADC5)			Purple
PC6		RESET

PD0		RXD
PD1		TXD
PD2		RoboCard USB D+ 	(INT0)
PD3		Motor left C 		(OC2B/INT1)
PD4		RoboCard USB D-		(T0)
PD5		Motor right EN 		(OC0B)
PD6		Motor left EN 		(OC0A)
PD7		RoboCard BL
